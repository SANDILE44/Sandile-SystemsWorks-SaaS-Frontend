Restaurant

A restaurant serving 200 customers daily at R250 average check can generate R1.5M monthly revenue yet still lose money when food costs, labour, and fixed expenses consume over 85% of every rand earned — leaving the owner with less than R15,000 profit after a full month of work. Sandile SystemsWorks shows your real profit, margin, and break-even before the month ends.

Manufacturing

A 5,000-unit production run priced to generate R420,000 revenue can hide R655,000 in total production costs — turning what looks like a profitable order into a R235,000 loss before the first unit is even produced. Sandile SystemsWorks exposes the real margin instantly so you know whether to proceed or walk away.

Construction

A contract worth R1.8M can appear profitable until labor overruns, subcontractor costs, and material volatility push total costs to R2.66M — creating a projected R860,000 loss. Sandile SystemsWorks calculates the true project margin before the contract is signed.

Logistics

A shipment invoiced at R120,000 may look profitable until fuel, driver pay, tolls, insurance, and empty return mileage reduce the margin to just 5% — leaving less than R6,000 profit on a high-risk job. Sandile SystemsWorks reveals the real shipment margin before accepting the load.
