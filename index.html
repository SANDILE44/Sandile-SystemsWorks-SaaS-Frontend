Simply input your data, and our calculators generate all critical business metrics instantly. No advanced skills are required, no complications, and results are immediately actionable.

From agriculture to IT services, consulting, and public administration, our calculators cover every sector, providing a full suite of tools to save time, improve decision-making, and maximize profits.

Primary Sector

Our calculators for agriculture, forestry, fisheries, mining, oil & gas, and renewable energy allow you to calculate everything from profitability and ROI to break-even, cost per unit, and revenue projections.

  • Agriculture: Farm profitability, societal revenue and costs, profit per animal, ROI, break-even units, cost per acre, livestock loss.
  • Forestry: Timber volume, revenue, costs, profit per meter, break-even volume, profit margin.
  • Fisheries: Net revenue, total costs, break-even, cash profits, monthly and annual revenue projections.
  • Mining & Oil/Gas: Monthly & annual revenue, total costs, profits, cost per ton, revenue per ton, ROI, profit margin.
  • Renewable Energy: Total costs, revenue, profits, ROI, annual payback, cost and revenue per kilowatt.

Secondary Sector

For manufacturing, construction, automotive, electronics, textiles, and food & beverage industries, our calculators provide precise production and financial metrics.

  • Units produced, total revenue, total cost, profit or loss, profit per unit.
  • Break-even units, cost per unit, revenue per unit, ROI, profit margin.
  • Monthly and annual revenue/profit projections.
  • Construction-specific: material, labor, equipment, fixed and social costs, profit ratios.
  • Food & Beverage & Textiles: gross and net profit, material cost per unit, monthly/annual projections.

Tertiary Sector

Service-based industries including real estate, retail, hospitality, restaurants, logistics, healthcare, education, finance, marketing, and consulting are fully covered.

  • Real estate: rental income, total expenses, net profit/loss, ROI, profit margins, monthly/annual profit.
  • Retail: total revenue, cost of goods sold, gross/net profits, markup, break-even units, profit per unit.
  • Restaurants: daily covers, revenue, total costs, profit margin, break-even cover, monthly/annual profits.
  • Hospitality: occupied rooms, revenue per room, cost per room, profit margin, ROI.
  • Logistics: monthly shipments, revenue per shipment, cost per shipment, ROI, profit margin.
  • Healthcare & Education: revenue, cost, profit per patient/student, ROI, profit margin.
  • Finance & Marketing & Consulting: client/project revenue, social cost, profit per client/campaign, ROI, break-even hours, monthly/annual profits.

Quaternary Sector

This includes IT & software, data science, cybersecurity, R&D, telecommunications, media, and SaaS subscription services.

  • IT & Software: revenue, costs, net profit, profit per hour, break-even hours, monthly & annual profits, ROI.
  • Media: revenue, social costs, cost per content, profit/loss, ROI, break-even content.
  • R&D: annual & monthly profits, net gains, ROI, payback period.
  • SaaS: monthly recurring revenue, net MRR, churn impact, social cost, ROI, profit margin, runway in months.
  • Telecommunications: subscribers, revenue, infrastructure & operating costs, net profit, ROI.

Quinary Sector

Government, NGOs, community services, and public administration calculators are designed for social and public projects.

  • Government & Public Admin: social program costs, purchase balances, staff costs, program efficiency.
  • NGOs: social costs, funds remaining/deficits, cost per program, impact efficiency.
  • Community Services: social participants, revenue, net impact/profit, ROI.
